Sailing trips from Cascais
Coastal Trips

Cruise along the coast, getting up close and personal to the rugged coastline exposed beyound Cascais Harbour. Bring your swimsuit for an oppertunity to cool off in the Atlantic Ocean!

Open Sea Trips

The monuments of Lisbon can be best seen from the open space of the river, get away from the crowds and experince the city from a more relaxed angle, soaking up the warm sun.

Set Sail

Seeing sail from Cascais in a beautiful and comfortable modern but tasteful sailing boat we will set sail west, heading out to the wil and exposed coastline on Portugals western edge. Here the scenery is beautiful and rugged with some amazing beaches to stop at and explore along the way. Exploring sea caves and cliffs such and Boca do Inferno is all possible within a short sail from Cascias.
