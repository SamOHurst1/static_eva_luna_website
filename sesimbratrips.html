Sailing trips from Sesimbra
Sail with Dolphins

Sesimbra is situated in the heart of Parque Natural da Arrábida, offering the chance to see a range of wildlife such as common and bottlenose dolphins straight out of the harbour!

Sailing and kayaking

The nearby coastline has huge rugged cliffs with crystal clear waters, perfect for snorkelling and kayaking from the boat. Secluded beaches offer the perfect spot to stop for lunch!

Set Sail

The coastline of the Parque Natural da Arrábida is an amzing area to sail and enjoy. The rugged cliffs offer breathtaking natural scenery with crystal clear waters at their base. We can bring kayaks and snorkelling equipment along for the tour for a chance to cool off and enjoy the blue waters. We can adapt our sailing venture from Sesimbra to make it a truly unforgetable experience enjoying one of the most beatiful stretches of coast in Portugal.
